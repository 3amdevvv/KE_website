About Us

Established in 2002, M/S KUNAL ENTERPRISES specializes in the supply and installation of electronic equipment for monitoring and controlling electrical power parameters. The proprietor, an electronics engineer from Pune (1988), has diverse experience with M/S FINOLEX CABLES LTD and TATA INSTITUTE OF FUNDAMENTAL RESEARCH. In 1993, the proprietor joined M/S MINILEC INDIA PVT LTD, advancing from Sales Engineer to Area Manager, overseeing regions such as Mumbai, Gujarat, and Asia Pacific Countries.
